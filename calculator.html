# Privacy Policy — RIG-PRO Scientific Calculator _Last updated: 4 July 2026_ RIG-PRO Scientific Calculator ("the app") is developed by RIG-PRO. This policy explains how the app handles your information. In short: **the app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.** ## Data we collect **None.** The app does not collect any personal or usage data. It has no analytics, no advertising, and no user accounts. ## Internet access The app requests **no permissions** and has **no internet access**. It cannot send any information off your device because it never connects to a network. ## Data stored on your device For your convenience, the app saves the following **locally on your device only** (it is never uploaded anywhere): - Your calculation history - The last answer (Ans) and memory (M) values - Your settings (theme, thousands separators, angle unit) This data stays on your device and is removed when you clear it in the app or uninstall the app. ## Children's privacy The app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any data from anyone, including children. ## Changes to this policy If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same location with a new "Last updated" date. ## Contact Questions about this policy?
